About Me

I'm Craig Mackenzie, a developer who specialises in building practical tools that solve real business problems. My focus is on automation, data analysis, and creating clean, functional web applications.

I work with small teams and business owners who need custom solutions but don't want the overhead of enterprise software. Whether it's streamlining a manual process, building a data dashboard, or creating a modern web presence, I deliver tools that are actually useful.

Based in Edinburgh, I combine technical expertise with clear communication and a focus on outcomes that matter to your business.
