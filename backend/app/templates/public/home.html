About Me

Automating real-world workflows and uncovering the insights behind the data.

My Background

I've been designing and building digital tools since I was a teenager, websites, small apps, automation scripts, whatever solved the problem in front of me. Most of my professional life has been in hospitality and customer facing roles, which means I understand how real businesses run and where the operational pain points actually are.

Finding The Problem

When I moved into sales administration, I saw the same problems everywhere: paper processes, duplicated work, and teams relying on memory instead of systems. So I built a full internal platform from scratch, automated workflows, a digital knowledge base, stock tracking, reporting, the works. Not because someone asked me to, but because the business needed it and I knew how to fix it.

A Pragmatic Approach

My focus is on transforming slow, manual workflows into clean, digital processes that save time and reduce errors. Using technologies like Python, Flask, and FastAPI, I design and build automation tools that help teams focus on high value work.

The goal is always the same: to build tools that remove friction, reveal what’s actually happening in the data, and make everyday work easier for the people using them.

I care about practical solutions, not buzzwords.
If a system doesn’t make life easier in the real world, it’s not finished.
