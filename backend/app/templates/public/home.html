About Me

Automating real-world workflows and uncovering the insights behind the data.

My Background

I've spent years dipping in and out of web development, Python, automation, and data analysis—always learning, always experimenting. It wasn't until I worked in a fast-paced sales and admin role that everything came together. Seeing how much time was lost to inefficient processes made it obvious how valuable the right systems could be.

So I built them. Automated workflows, digital admin tools, internal dashboards, knowledge bases—simple systems that replaced hours of manual work and gave the team clearer visibility into what was actually happening.

That's what I focus on now: creating practical, data-driven tools for businesses that want to move away from pen-and-paper workflows and start operating with modern, efficient systems.

What I Do

Workflow Automation

I design simple, reliable digital tools that remove slow, manual steps from day-to-day work. My focus is always the same: reduce friction, increase clarity, and give teams back their time.

  • • Replace paper processes with fast digital workflows
  • • Automate repetitive admin and communication loops

Data Intelligence

I help businesses understand what their data is actually saying. Instead of scattered spreadsheets, I build clear, practical insights that guide smarter decisions.

  • • Usage analytics and performance reports
  • • Simple dashboards that highlight trends and issues

Custom Business Systems

I build tailored systems that match how a business really works—not generic off-the-shelf tools. Everything is designed to be clear, efficient, and easy for non-technical teams.

  • • Bring multiple workflows into one clean hub
  • • Scale as the business grows without adding complexity
